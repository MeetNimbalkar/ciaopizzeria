Our Story

WHERE ITALY MEETS
Ahmedabad

Born from a deep love for wood-fired traditions and the vibrant soul of Gujarat, Ciao Pizzeria opened its doors with one sacred promise — to serve the most authentic Neapolitan pizza this side of Naples. We use hand-stretched sourdough bases, San Marzano tomatoes, and freshly imported Italian cheeses, all kissed by the flames of our 450°C stone oven.

From casual lunches to late-night slices, Ciao is your neighbourhood Italian escape — loud, warm, and unapologetically delicious. Bring your appetite, your people, and maybe a belt with a few extra holes.
